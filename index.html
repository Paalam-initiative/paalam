Discover Our Work

Our Vision

We act as the vital connector between deep-rooted silence and the essential knowledge required for menstrual health and dignity. Our goal is to transform menstruation from a source of shame into a recognized standard of health and public dignity.

The Challenge

In many regions across India—particularly within rural and underserved communities—menstruation remains shrouded in a "culture of silence." When the topic is taboo, knowledge remains inaccessible, leading to improper hygiene practices, school dropouts, and long-term health complications.

If not us, who? If not now, when?

The Three-Pillar Framework

To create lasting systemic change, Paalam operates through a multi-dimensional approach:

1. Community-Centric Education

The Knowledge Bridge

We facilitate immersive awareness sessions in schools, orphanages, and old-age homes to challenge cultural narratives that sustain stigma.

2. Resource Equity & Distribution

The Access Bridge

We lead strategic fundraising campaigns to procure and distribute sanitary products, ensuring "period poverty" never blocks education.

3. Narrative Shifting & Digital Advocacy

The Awareness Bridge

Through high-impact digital campaigns, we dismantle myths and build an ecosystem where menstrual health is discussed openly.

Marketing Team: The Voice of Paalam

Our Marketing Team shapes how the world sees and hears us. As the drivers of Paalam's media presence, they ensure our message breaks through the silence. Composed of creative minds, trend-spotters, and video/reel editors, this team brainstorms fresh content and sparks meaningful online conversations to expand our reach. Their creativity is our megaphone.

Design Team: The Visionaries

Our Design Team acts as the guardians of Paalam's visual and brand identity. Because art is a powerful tool for advocacy, this team creates everything from striking social media posts to engaging presentation decks that directly challenge the stigma. Using Canva and advanced design programs, they are the visionaries who paint our mission into reality.

Outreach Team: The Bridge Builders

These are Paalam's bridge builders (literally!). As our chief connection-makers, the Outreach Team is the vital link between our mission and the community. They actively research and build relationships with NGOs, orphanages, and schools. By reaching out, pitching our mission, and securing spaces for our sessions, they build the very bridges we walk across.

Education Team: The Heartbeat of Paalam

Our Education Team provides the boots on the ground, turning our mission into direct action. Driven by empathy and boldness, they are at the forefront—conducting our sessions, breaking down complex concepts, and ensuring audiences feel seen and comfortable. From researching session topics to building out our curriculum, they are the catalysts for lasting, intergenerational change.
