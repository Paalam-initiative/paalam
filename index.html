Paalam

Malayalam for "Bridge"


A youth-led initiative dedicated to dismantling the culture of silence and systemic stigma surrounding menstrual hygiene in India.

Discover Our Work

Our Vision

We act as the vital connector between deep-rooted silence and the essential knowledge required for menstrual health and dignity. Our goal is to transform menstruation from a source of shame into a recognized standard of health and public dignity.

The Challenge

In many regions across India—particularly within rural and underserved communities—menstruation remains shrouded in a "culture of silence." When the topic is taboo, knowledge remains inaccessible, leading to improper hygiene practices, school dropouts, and long-term health complications.

If not us, who? If not now, when?

The Three-Pillar Framework

To create lasting systemic change, Paalam operates through a multi-dimensional approach:

1. Community-Centric Education

The Knowledge Bridge

We facilitate immersive awareness sessions in schools, orphanages, and old-age homes to challenge cultural narratives that sustain stigma.

2. Resource Equity & Distribution

The Access Bridge

We lead strategic fundraising campaigns to procure and distribute sanitary products, ensuring "period poverty" never blocks education.

3. Narrative Shifting & Digital Advocacy

The Awareness Bridge

Through high-impact digital campaigns, we dismantle myths and build an ecosystem where menstrual health is discussed openly.
